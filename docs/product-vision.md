# AXOS OS - Product Vision

AXOS OS is not just an inventory app; it is an **Industrial Operating System** for manufacturing companies.

## The Pillars
1. **ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning)**: Management of customers, users, inventory, materials, orders, costs, and reports.
2. **MES (Manufacturing Execution System)**: Real-time control of production lines, models, BOM, kitting, quality, and downtime.
3. **Control Tower**: A centralized dashboard for alerts, KPIs, paros de línea risk, and traceability.
4. **AI Layer**: Predictive analytics for shortages, kitting optimization, and operational analysis.

## Aesthetic & Experience
- **Minimalist**: Clean interfaces with high whitespace.
- **Premium Industrial**: Inspired by Apple, Tesla, and modern SAP.
- **Visuals**: Dark mode excellence, soft cards, serious typography, discreet animations.
- **Semáforos Operativos**: Clear visual indicators of status (Green/Yellow/Red) but in a sophisticated palette.
