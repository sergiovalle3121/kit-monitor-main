# Quality Command Center Roadmap

The `/dashboard/quality` cockpit is the operational entry point for the existing AXOS quality stack. It does not replace NCR, CTQ, measurements, floor quality, holds, RMA, genealogy, inspections, defect codes, or analytics; it organizes them into one command-center view for EMS quality teams.

## Existing foundations

- NCR lifecycle: `/ncr` supports the main no-conformance list, detail, status flow, and classification work.
- Quality analytics: `/quality/analytics` aggregates defect Pareto, supplier/process PPM, FPY, OQC yield, CAPA, model/line cuts, and disposition summaries. `/quality/analytics/command-center` now adds operational lanes, attention items, containment scope, MRB disposition math, and CTQ readiness in one backend response.
- CTQ and SPC foundation: `/quality/characteristics`, `/quality/measurements`, and `/quality/measurements/summary` provide critical characteristics, readings, LSL/Nominal/USL context, and out-of-spec summaries.
- MRB and containment: `/floor-quality/holds`, `/floor-quality/kpis`, `/floor-quality/where-used`, `/quality/holds/active`, `/quality/dispositions`, and `/quality/transfers` cover floor holds, inventory holds, disposition, quarantine movement, and where-used lookup.
- Customer and traceability loops: `/rma`, `/rma/kpis`, `/genealogy`, and `/genealogy/kpis` already cover RMA/customer quality, serial/lot traceability, and recall-readiness signals.

## Implemented in this phase

- Premium hero that frames quality as an end-to-end EMS command center.
- Operational KPI strip using NCR, quality analytics, and floor-quality KPI endpoints with graceful empty states.
- Horizontal quality flow rail from IQC through RMA/Genealogy.
- Attention queue that surfaces critical NCRs, overdue CAPA signals, MRB holds, and uncontained NCRs.
- Risk panels for repeated defects, supplier PPM risk, and model/line concentration.
- Organized module grid linking to existing quality routes instead of creating parallel screens.
- Customer-quality and traceability closed-loop panel using RMA and genealogy KPI endpoints.
- War room operativo por proceso (IQC, IPQC, MRB, SPC/CTQ, CAPA, OQC/customer) with containment, escalation, and disposition guidance.
- Containment scope builder for lots, serials, work orders, customers, and high-risk NCRs.
- MRB disposition cockpit that compares affected vs dispositioned units and documents practical disposition playbooks.
- Backend `/quality/analytics/command-center` endpoint for server-side war-room derivations, reducing the amount of brittle client-only orchestration.
- Command-center backend now joins floor-quality, RMA, and genealogy services for MRB KPIs, customer loop KPIs, and traceability coverage in the same summary payload.
- Operational drilldowns for supplier risk, line/model risk, containment candidates, customer/shipment exposure, and CAPA aging. These are generated by the command-center backend and rendered in `/dashboard/quality` as the action layer between the war room and the customer traceability loop.
- Today's quality battle rhythm: server-derived handoff cards for production blockers, shipment blockers, supplier containment, release candidates, and owner load.
- Action launcher plan: read-only server-generated action candidates with endpoint, permission, payload template, prechecks, and audit trail for holds, MRB, SCAR prep, recall scope, and customer 8D.
- Aging and escalation control: command-center age buckets, static SLA policies by source/severity, SLA breach list, stale NCR list, and owner escalations to prevent critical or old NCRs from sitting without ownership.
